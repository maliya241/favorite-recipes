Baked Chicken Breasts

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breasts
  • Equal Parts of:
    • Garlic Powder
    • Onion Powder
    • Salt
    • Black Pepper

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375℉ to bake for 40 minutes or preheat oven to 400℉ to bake for 30 minutes.
  2. Mix garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and black pepper and rub on chicken.
  3. Bake chicken.
