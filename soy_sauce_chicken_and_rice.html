Soy Sauce Chicken and Rice

Recipe Multiplier

1

Ingredients

  • 1/8 cup Sugar *
  • 1/8 cup Soy Sauce *
  • 1 1/2 cups Water *
  • 1 teaspoon Salt *
  • 3/8 teaspoon Garlic Powder *
  • 1/8 teaspoon Ground Ginger *
  • 1 Chicken Breast *
  • 1/2 cup Rice, uncooked *

Directions

  1. Combine sugar, soy sauce, water, salt, garlic powder, and ground ginger in a pan; stir until sugar is dissolved.
  2. Place chicken in pan and bring to a boil.
  3. Stir in rice. Cover the pan.
  4. Reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes or until all water is absorbed and chicken is cooked.

Notes

  • * Changed from Original Recipe.
  • Not good leftover.
