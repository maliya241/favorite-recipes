Yogurt Chicken

Recipe Multiplier

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless boneless Chicken Breasts
  • 3/4 cup Plain Low-Fat Yogurt
  • 1 Lemon, juiced
  • 1 cup Breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 teaspoon Garlic Powder
  • Salt

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350℉.
  2. Place yogurt in a small bowl and stir until creamy smooth.
  3. Add lemon juice with the creamy smooth yogurt and stir together.
  4. In another small bowl, combine the breadcrumbs, garlic powder, and salt; mix together.
  5. Rinse chicken breats and pat dry.
  6. Dip each breast in the lemon and yogurt mixture, then in the breadcrumb mixture.
  7. Bake for 1 hour.
  8. Let cool for 5 to 10 minutes before serving.
