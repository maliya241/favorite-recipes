Pie Crust

Recipe Multiplier

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 cup All Purpose Flour
  • 1/2 cup Butter
  • 1 tablespoon Sugar
  • 1/8 cup Water, icy cold

Directions

  1. Mix together 1 cup of flour and butter (chopped if using food processor or microwaved for 30 seconds and broken up with whisk before mixing with flour).
  2. Once blended together, add the remaining flour and sugar.
  3. Mix in the icy cold water until well blended together.
  4. Roll out the needed size, and it is ready to use.
