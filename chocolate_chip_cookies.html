Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Butter
  • 1/2 cup Granulated Sugar
  • 1/4 cup Brown Sugar, packed
  • 2 teaspoons Vanilla Extract
  • 1 large Egg
  • 1 1/4 cups All-Purpose Flour *
  • 1/2 teaspoon Baking Soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon Kosher Salt
  • 1/2 cup Semisweet Chocolate Chips *

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350℉.
  2. Microwave the butter for about 40 seconds. Butter should be completely metled but shouldn't be hot.
  3. In a large bowl, mix butter with the sugars until well-combined.
  4. Stir in vanilla and egg until incorporated.
  5. Add the flour, baking soda, and salt.
  6. Mix dough until just combined. Dough should be soft and a little sticky but not overly sticky.
  7. Stir in chocolate chips.
  8. Scoop out 1.5 tablespoons of dough (medium cookie scoop) and place 2 inches apart on baking sheet.
  9. Bake for 7-10 minutes, or until cookies are set. They will be puffy and still look a little underbaked in the middle.

Notes

  • * Changed from Original Recipe
  • They do not spread much if use original amount of flour.
  • Using plain yogurt instead of butter makes them cakey.
  • Using 1/3 cup of vegetable oil and 2 tablespoons of water instead of butter makes them cakey as well.
