Homemade Pasta Dough

Recipe Multiplier

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Flour
  • 3 Eggs, room temperature
  • 1 tablespoon Oil
  • 1 teaspoon Salt

Directions

  1. Begin your pasta by taking flour and pouring it on your surface. Make a well in the flour.
  2. Add your eggs to the well.
  3. Add olive oil and salt to the well.
  4. Use chopsticks to combine the ingredients. Start by breaking the egg yokes and then slowly stir to incorporate flour.
  5. Knead until stretchy, smooth, and firm.
  6. Cover in plastic and let rest for about 30 minutes.
  7. Remove pasta and roll out on a floured surface.
  8. Shape pasta. More on pasta shapes linked below.
  9. Boil water in a pot. Add enough salt to make it salty like the ocean.
  10. Cook pasta.
  11. Make pasta sauce.
