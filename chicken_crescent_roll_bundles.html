Chicken Crescent Roll Bundles

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Cooked Rotisserie Chicken
  • 2 cans Cream of Chicken Soup
  • 1 package Ranch Dressing Mix
  • 2 8 oz cans Crescent Roll Dough
  • 1 8 oz package Cream Cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375℉.
  2. Add cream cheese and both cans of soup to a medium pot and turn to medium heat. Stir until cream cheese melts.
  3. Add dressing mix and stir until well combined. Turn down to low heat.
  4. Pour most of the mixture into a large bowl, leaving enough in the pan to use as a gravy to put on the chicken bundles.
  5. Add the chicken to the large bowl and mix together.
  6. Separate the crescent roll dough and place individual triangles on a cookie sheet.
  7. Scoop a spoonful of chicken mix onto the crescent roll dough and roll (fold the left side in, right side in, and then roll towards the long point).
  8. Bake in the oven for 9 - 12 minutes.
