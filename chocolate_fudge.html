Chocolate Fudge

Ingredients

  • 3 cups Sugar
  • 1 1/2 sticks Margarine
  • 12 oz Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips
  • 6 1/2 oz Marshmallow Creme
  • 1 tsp Vanilla
  • 1/2 and 1/3 cup Evaporated Milk

Directions

  1. Stir together margarine, sugar, and milk in a heavy 5 quart pan; bring to full boil stirring constantly.
  2. Boil 5 minutes over medium heat, stirring constantly to prevent scorching.
  3. Remove from heat.
  4. Gradually stir in chocolate chips until melted.
  5. Add remaining ingredients; mix until well blended.
  6. Pour into two greased 9 inch or 1 13x9 inch baking pan.
  7. Cool at room temperature.
  8. Cut into squares.
