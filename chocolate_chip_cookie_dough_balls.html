Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough Balls

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup Butter, Softened
  • 1/3 cup Brown Sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons Vanilla Extract
  • 1 cup All Purpose Flour
  • 1-2 teaspoon Milk, as needed
  • 1 cup Mini Milk Chocolate Chips
  • 2 cup Milk Chocolate or Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips (for melting)

Directions

  1. In a mixing bowl, beat together the butter, brown sugar, and vanilla.
  2. Stir in the flour, just until combined.
  3. If the dough is dry, add 1-2 tsp of milk.
  4. Stir in mini chocolate chips.
  5. Grease hands with cooking spray and roll the dough into 20 small balls (about 1 inch).
  6. Refrigerate for 30 minutes, or freeze for 15.
