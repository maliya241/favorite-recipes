Breadcrumbs

Ingredients

  • bread

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350℉.
  2. Tear bread into 1-2" chunks.
  3. Spread bread chunks out into a single layer over a large baking sheet.
  4. Bake bread chunks, stirring every five minutes or so, until they are dry and just starting to turn a little golden on the edges (about 15 - 20 minutes).
  5. Once dry, remove them from the oven and let them cool.
  6. Once dried and cooled, make the bread chunks into crumbs by using a food processor or doing it manually by crushing with a rolling pin. Crushing bread chunks manually could smush the bread instead of crumnling because it's not completely dry.
  7. Once the bread crumbs are made, use immediately or store in an air tight zip lock bag in the freezer.
