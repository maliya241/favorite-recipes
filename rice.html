Rice

Recipe Multiplier

1

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Rice
  • 1 cup Water
Directions
  1. In a sauce pan, bring water to a boil.
  2. Stir in rice.
  3. Cover. Reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes or until all water is absorbed.

Notes

  • For every cup of rice, add double the amount of water.
  • For drier rice, use 1/4 cup less water.
  • For moister rice, use 1/4 cup more water.
