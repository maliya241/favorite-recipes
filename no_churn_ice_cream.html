No Churn Ice Cream

Recipe Multiplier

1

Ingredients

Ice Cream Base

  • 1 (14 oz) can Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • pinch of Salt
  • 2 cups cold Heavy Cream

Vanilla Ice Cream

  • 2 teaspoons Pure Vanilla Extract or Vanilla Bean Paste

Chocolate Ice Cream

  • 1/8 cup Unsweetened Cocoa Powder

Directions

  1. Whisk together the condensed milk, salt, and flavoring ingredient in a large bowl.
  2. Whip the heavy cream with a mixer on medium-high speed until firm peaks form, about 2 minutes.
  3. Fold about 1 cup of the whipped cream into the condensed milk mixture with a rubber spatula until combined.
  4. Then fold the lightened mixture into the whipped cream until well blended.
  5. Pour into a freezer safe container and cover it. Put into freezer.
  6. For mix-ins, freeze for about 2 hours and swirl in any desired mix-ins with a spoon.
  7. Freeze covered for at least 5 hours.
