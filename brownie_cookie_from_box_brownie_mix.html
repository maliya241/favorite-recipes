Brownie Cookies from Box Brownie Mix

Ingredients

  • 1 box (18 oz) Box Brownie Mix
  • 1/2 cup All Purpose Flour
  • 2 large Eggs
  • 5 tablespoons Vegetable Oil
  • 1 tablespoon Water
  • 1/2 cup Chocolate Chips

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350℉ and line a large baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, combine all of the ingredients and mix well, scraping down the sides as needed. The dough will be very sticky.
  3. Scoop out the dough and drop onto the prepared baking sheet about 2 in apart.
  4. Bake for 7-8 minutes.
  5. Remove from oven and allow to cool on pan for a couple of minutes before transferring to a cooling rack.
  6. Consume or store in an air-tight container for up to one week.
