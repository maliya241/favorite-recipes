Sweetened Condensed Milk

Ingredients

  • 1 (12 oz) can Evaporated Milk
  • 1/2 to 1 cup Sugar
  • 1 tablespoon Butter

Directions

  1. Place the milk and sugar in a pan.
  2. Turn on medium-high heat.
  3. Bring to a boil, stirring frequently.
  4. Turn heat down a bit and keep at a slow rolling boil for about 10 minutes. Continue stirring frequently.
  5. Take off heat and add butter. Stir butter to melt and let cool.
