Cream Cheese Pasta

Recipe Multiplier

1

Ingredients

  • 1/4 pound Pasta *
  • 2 ounces Cream Cheese *
  • 1/4 cup Grated Parmesan *
  • 1/16 teaspoon Garlic Powder *
  • 1/16 teaspoon Onion Powder *
  • 1/8 teaspoon Black Pepper *
  • 1/8 cup Milk **
  • 1/4 cup Cooking Water *

Directions

  1. In a large pot of boiling salted water, cook pasta according to package directions until al dente.
  2. Right before draining, reserve a cup of starchy pasta water. Drain the pasta and set aside.
  3. Add the cream cheese, starchy pasta water, garlic powder, onion powder, black pepper, and milk in pot. Stir to combine and create a nice and creamy emulsion. Add parmesan cheese and stir well. Your pasta sauce is ready.
  4. Add the pasta back into the pot, and stir well to evenly coat the pasta in the sauce, add more cooking water as needed for a smooth and creamy consistency.

Notes

  • * Changed from Original Recipe.
  • ** Added ingredient.
