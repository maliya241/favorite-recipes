Chocolate Fudge Pie

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup Unsalted Butter, melted
  • 1 1/2 cups Granulated Sugar
  • 1/3 cup All Purpose Flour
  • 1/8 teaspoon Salt
  • 1/3 cup Cocoa
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons Vanilla Extract
  • 3 large Eggs
  • 1 9-inch Pie Shell, unbaked

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350℉.
  2. Melt butter in a large microwave safe bowl. Add sugar to melted butter and use a hand mixer or whisk to combine until sugar is mostly dissolved.
  3. Add remaining ingredients and beat or whisk until well blended.
  4. Pour into pie crust and bake 35-40 minutes, or until center is set and toothpick has gooey wet crumbs. Make sure you don't overbake.
