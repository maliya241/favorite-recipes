Chocolate Tart

Recipe Multiplier

1

Ingredients

  • 400 grams (about 2 1/2 cups) Cooking Chocolate Chips
  • 150 milliliters Heavy Cream

Directions

  1. Put chocolate chips and heavy cream in a bowl and melt at 30 second intervals until completely melted (about 3 rounds).
  2. Pour the melted mix into something and refrigerate until solid.
