Potato Peelings Chips

Recipe Multiplier

1

Ingredients

  • Peelings from 2 Potatoes
  • 2 teaspoons Oil
  • Pinch of Salt

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425. Line a rimmed baking sheet with parchment paper or non-stick foil.
  2. Gently toss the peelings with the oil. Place in a single layer on the baking sheet, and sprinkle with salt.
  3. Bake for about 15 to 20 minutes or so, watching carefully through the door to make sure they donâ€™t get too brown. Remove when they are crispy and serve at once.
