Fluffy Chocolate Frosting

Recipe Multiplier

Ingredients

  • 6 tablespoons Margarine, cold *
  • 6 tablespoons Water, hot
  • 6 tablespoons Cocoa Powder
  • 1 teaspoon Vanilla
  • 4 cups Powdered Sugar

Directions

  1. Place water in a microwave-safe bowl and heat until it just begins to boil, approximately 1 1/2 minutes.
  2. Add cocoa powder to the hot water. Set aside.
  3. Place cold margarine in a large bowl. Use a hand mixer and mix for 1 – 2 minutes until it begins to soften.
  4. Add the vanilla to the margarine. Mix well.
  5. Add 2 cups of powdered sugar to the margarine and mix well.
  6. Add the cocoa mixture to the bowl and mix well.
  7. Slowly add the remaining powdered sugar until you reach the desired consistency.
  8. Whip the frosting mixture for 1 more minute.
  9. Then use the icing to decorate your cake or cupcakes.

Notes

  • * For dairy-free frosting, use dairy-free margarine.
  • If your frosting is not as stiff as you would like it, then add more powdered sugar.
  • If your frosting is too thick add one teaspoon of cold water. Mix well. Repeat as needed. Do not add more than a teaspoon of water at a time because your frosting can quickly go from too thick to too thin.
  • If your frosting is too sweet, add a pinch of salt.
